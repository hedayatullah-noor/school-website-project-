ABOUT SCHOOL

Rising stars academy, is an English medium,islamic, Co-Educational School, that was founded in the year 2008. The school has around 2500 students and has quickly become one of the most reputed schools in the area

The sprawling 3 acres campus of our school comprises a 100000 sq. ft. built up area and a 2 acres Sports & Games Ground. There are three buildings equipped with all the amenities to facilitate all round development for our students. The campus has a lot of open space which is lined with trees and plants to enhance the serenity. This state-of -the-art infrastructure makes RSA the most befitting place to joyously spend ones SCHOOL LIFE !!

Our system of imparting education employs a healthy amalgamation of traditional methods and modern techniques. We strive to make school life a wonderful experience and work with the motto : "Education Towards Enlightenment"
